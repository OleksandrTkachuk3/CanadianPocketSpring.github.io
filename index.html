Pocket Spring

A pocket spring mattress is made from individual pocket springs. Each pocket spring is enclosed within its own fabric pocket. Working independently from each other, each spring reacts only to the pressure applied to that area.
A good mattress with pocket springs guarantees a peaceful and comfortable sleep, which can lead to an energetic and successful day. Pocket springs are manufactured with advanced technology, where each spring is individually encased in a fabric pocket and operates independently. This feature prevents movement in one part of the mattress from transferring to other parts, allowing you to sleep comfortably without disturbing your partner.
